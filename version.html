-40m

-20m
REFEREES perform official field check
-15m
REFEREES collect OFFICIAL PRINTED rosters from both teams
-12m

⚠️ Teams may be fined if match starts late. Forfeit if not started 15 min after kickoff — responsible team pays full ref fees.
📋 Game Setup Requirements
  • ALL players must show photo ID or team page photo. No ID = No Play, no exceptions.
  • Mark off proper technical area with cones if necessary
  • BOTH TEAMS ON SAME SIDE OF FIELD
  • Only 20 players + coaching staff allowed in technical area
  • Non-playing roster players must be with fans, not in technical area
  • If NOT enclosed: ALL fans/spectators on opposite side of field
  • HOME team provides 3 official SWPL/Xara game balls & 4 corner flags
  • HOME team MUST FILM/RECORD the entire match with sound
  • VISITING team changes kit or wears pennies in case of color conflict
  • Both teams' subs wear pennies on bench

📄 Official Match Roster
  • DIGITAL COPY NOT ACCEPTED. Both teams must bring a printed copy.
  • If no official roster is available, the game is cancelled.
  • Roster must feature the SWPL logo and include: both team names, date, time, and location.
  • Referees must write all officials' names on the report.
  • Up to 20 players may be checked in per team on gameday.

✅ Check-In Procedure
  1. All check-ins must occur at least 10 minutes before kickoff.
  2. Collect printed match reports from both teams.
  3. Collect referee fees. DO NOT START UNTIL BOTH TEAMS HAVE PAID IN FULL.
  4. Verify coach/manager identity — one staff per team prints name and signs post-match.
  5. Verify every player: match PHOTO ID to face, ask player to state DOB aloud, verify jersey number, mark "ChkIn" box. Cross out absent players.
✏️ Write-In Players
  • Write-ins allowed — verify name and photo ID
  • MUST include DOB; name must be fully legible
  • SWPL staff will validate eligibility
  • ⚠️ Writing in a player risks forfeit and a $75 fine for illegal player

⏰ Start Time & Grace Period
  • Both teams have 7+ players: NO grace period. Game starts on time.
  • If one/both teams lack 7 players: 15-minute grace period from scheduled kickoff.
  • Once 7th player checks in, game begins immediately.
  • After 15 min grace with fewer than 6 players: referee whistles final. No ref fees collected — referees invoice league.
  • If game plays after grace period, it is official — no protest accepted.
  • Grace period time does NOT reduce match length. Full 90 min still played.

📝 Referee Post-Game
  1. Mark final score at top of match report
  2. Mark goals scored at bottom — must indicate player number
  3. Mark yellow and red cards next to player names
  4. Present completed report to both managers to SIGN
  5. Managers may take a photo of the completed and signed report
  6. Within 24 hours: email to matchreport@swplsoccer.com AND your assignor
  7. Red cards/disruptions: submit supplemental report within 24 hours to same email
🏆 Club Staff Post-Game
  1. Sign the match roster — confirm stats before signing
  2. Text final score to (562) 537-1472 OR post on WhatsApp group
  3. Login to account. Record full match stats (see tutorial videos folder)
  4. Fill out Post Match Report Survey — incidents, standards, referee review
  5. Post on team social media — results, highlights, upcoming match
  6. Submit Player of the Week nomination
  7. Upload match footage to your website/YouTube channel
  8. Submit match link & photos to dennis@swplsoccer.com
