Who we are

Built for players who want more.

GB United is a competitive youth soccer club based in Sherman Oaks, Los Angeles. We give players a club home where development comes first, with professional coaching, position specific training programs, and a club culture families are proud to be part of.

We compete in the Coast Soccer League across boys and girls age groups, with two training sessions per week, individual player reviews, and a full year of competitive soccer.
