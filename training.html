Weekly structure

How training works.

2x

Team training

Every team trains twice per week for 90 minutes. Structured sessions with your team coach, focused on technical development and team shape.

GK

Goalkeeper sessions

Bi-weekly dedicated Goalkeeper training with Aidan Feuer. Goalkeepers get their own coaching space to develop the skills specific to their position.

ST

Striker sessions

Bi-weekly Striker training with Thomas Elliott. Finishing, movement, and decision-making. The things that turn good players into goal scorers.
